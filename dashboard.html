Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI platforms are supported?
Currently supports ChatGPT (openai.com/chatgpt.com), Deepseek (deepseek.com), Kimi (kimi.moonshot.cn), and Google AI Studio (aistudio.google.com).
Storage quantity limits?
The current demo version supports unlimited entries; to ensure search latency < 1 second, it is recommended to keep active entries under 10,000.
Length restrictions
Single content is recommended not to exceed 50,000 characters; if it does, it can be split and saved separately.
Positioning issues
When AI websites use lazy loading or DOM structure updates, positioning will fall back to the top of the session, which is an expected protection mechanism.
Synchronization speed
All data is stored locally in IndexedDB, and synchronization speed depends on your local hardware performance.
